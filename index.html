About me

I'm a full stack developer based near Bath, in Wiltshire. Originally a bookseller, I made the choice to change careers into something I feel more passionate about (and I was already very invested in the bookselling world!).

I graduated from the Full Stack Track at iO Academy and I'm now a full stack developer at Glentham Life Sciences, specialising in Django, systems architecture and production ownership of internal business platforms. I design and deploy the tools that replace manual, paper-based workflows across warehouse and logistics operations, from dispatch systems used on warehouse scanners to automated stock sync pipelines and operational dashboards.

I hold end-to-end responsibility for the stack, backend development, deployment infrastructure and dependency/security maintenance, and I'm still keen to sink my teeth into any project and relish the opportunity to learn new languages and frameworks.

Historical Stock Tracking & IST Dispatch

Django Celery Redis JavaScript MariaDB

As part of the Orders and Logistics Activity Dashboard (OLAD) I own at Glentham Life Sciences, I created a historical stock tracking algorithm that syncs regularly via Celery using a flagging system, alongside an IST dispatch system used on warehouse scanners. Together these have improved warehouse output from a maximum of 2-3 International Stock Transfers a week up to 7. They have also levelled the business skill, so a once complicated process restricted to the warehouse manager can now be done by every warehouse user.

Internal business system, not publicly accessible.
